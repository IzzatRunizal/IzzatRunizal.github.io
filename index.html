What is Modul.

Modul. is a graphic design tool used to create or modify our style of invitation card, our company provide 3 types of invitation card which is Formal, Informal and Corporate invitation. Our website offers several features to make user reach out to their ideal type of invitation card. The software is free to use and would be suitable for a beginner user.

Formal

Our company provide two types of a formal invitation. Farewell, party and wedding invitation are standby by us for a user to use. The templates can be edit as we serve a few features like changing colour background, drag and drop and editable content.

Informal

We include two types of invitation cards in these informal invitation cards: Eid al-Fitr Mubarak invitation card template and birthday card template invitations. Users can edit and change these invitation cards utilizing drag and drop for the animation that our company provides. The sentences, for example, can be chosen and dragged to the cards.

Corporate

Our organization produced two invitation cards for this corporate workshop invitation cards: a self-development workshop program and a business meeting workshop program. We created drag and drop animation for both invitation cards that can be easily used by users such as dropping the emotes sticker to the cards, changing the background colour and editable content.
